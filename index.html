About Me


Hi, my name is Caleb Christensen and I am a Javascript Developer. I'm 28 and ready to start the next chapter of my life. I was born in Indiana and raised in Michigan, however I live in Fishers, Indiana. I have a wife and three beautiful kids who are my world. My sister, a full stack developer, inspired me to chase my dream of working in the tech world. I am passionate about brainstorming, planning, designing, executing, and improving projects.

I started off as a young entrepreneur at the age of 20. For the past 8 years I have been a partner and general manager of two Jet's Pizza locations in Indiana.
